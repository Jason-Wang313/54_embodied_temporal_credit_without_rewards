# Reviewer Attacks

## Attack: Raw traces fail when events are misbound.

Sustained historically and preserved. The final method audits binding and reports fallback, abstention, and miscredit.

## Attack: Dense shaping can be better under adversarial events.

Sustained on localization error. The final paper reports this and explains that dense shaping carries reward dependence and proxy regret.

## Attack: The method wins by abstaining.

Addressed. Fallback is 0.242278 and abstention is 0.033219; both are reported.

## Attack: This is not real robot evidence.

Sustained as a limitation. The final claim is a deterministic benchmark and reporting discipline, not deployment safety.

## Attack: Reward-free does not mean label-free.

Addressed. The paper explicitly states that event traces are local physical labels, not scalar rewards.
